圣骑士 Paladin

Holy Warrior · Tank & Support

🛡️ 定位：坦克 / 辅助 Role: Tank / Support

圣骑士是憎恨之王扩展包中加入的近战战士职业，擅长使用神圣力量与盾牌进行防御与攻击。他们拥有独特的光环系统，能够为团队提供强大的增益效果，同时具备出色的单体与群体伤害能力。作为团队的核心支柱，圣骑士在组队内容中尤为受欢迎。 The Paladin is a melee warrior class wielding holy power and shields. With their unique Aura system, they provide powerful buffs to the team while dealing solid single-target and AoE damage. As the team's backbone, Paladins are especially popular in group content.

核心技能 Core Skills

完整技能树 Full Skill Tree

防护光环 Guardian Aura
Defense

为周围队友提供15%减伤，持续8秒。升级后增加范围与减伤比例。 Grant nearby allies 15% damage reduction for 8s. Upgrades increase range and reduction.

力量光环 Might Aura
Defense

为周围队友增加20%攻击强度，持续10秒。可与防护光环叠加。 Increase nearby allies' attack power by 20% for 10s. Stacks with Guardian Aura.

净化光环 Cleansing Aura
Utility

每秒清除范围内队友的1个负面状态，并持续恢复生命值。 Remove 1 debuff per second from nearby allies and continuously restore HP.

盾牌格挡 Shield Block
Defense

主动技能：举起盾牌格挡下一次攻击，成功格挡后反击造成150%伤害并晕眩敌人1秒。 Active: Block the next attack. Counter-attack deals 150% damage and stuns for 1s.

圣光爆发 Holy Burst
Offense

主要输出技能。对前方扇形范围造成神圣伤害，命中减速目标时伤害提高40%。 Primary damage skill. Deals holy damage in a frontal cone. +40% damage vs slowed targets.

神圣打击 Divine Smite
Offense

单体高伤害技能。对单一目标造成300%武器伤害，并附加圣火持续伤害。 Single-target skill dealing 300% weapon damage plus holy fire DoT.

庇护之光 Sanctuary Light
Defense

团队治疗技能。为范围内所有队友恢复30%最大生命值，并在地上留下治疗圈持续5秒。 Heal all allies in range for 30% max HP and leave a healing zone for 5s.

正义之锤 Hammer of Justice
Utility

范围晕眩技能。跳跃至目标位置，对周围敌人造成伤害并晕眩2秒。 Leap to target location, damage and stun surrounding enemies for 2s.

技能加点优先级 Skill Point Priority

  • 1 前期：圣光爆发 + 盾牌格挡（核心输出与生存） Early: Holy Burst + Shield Block (core damage & survival)
  • 2 中期：防护光环 + 力量光环（团队辅助能力解锁） Mid: Guardian Aura + Might Aura (team support unlocked)
  • 3 后期：神圣打击 + 庇护之光 + 正义之锤（完整技能循环） Late: Divine Smite + Sanctuary Light + Hammer of Justice

推荐BD流派 Recommended Builds

🛡️ 光环护卫流

以三大光环为核心，定位纯辅助。最大化团队减伤与攻强加成，在高层大秘境中保证队友生存。适合组队玩家。 Three-aura focused pure support build. Maximize team damage reduction and attack buffs. Ideal for high-tier group content.

⚔️ 破盾圣战流

盾牌格挡反击为核心输出手段，兼顾伤害与控制。高冷却缩减保证频繁反击，晕眩链控住精英怪，适合单刷与组队。 Shield block counter as main damage source with solid control. High CDR ensures frequent counters. Good for solo and groups.

术士 Warlock

Demon Conjurer · Melee Summoner

🗡️ 定位：近战召唤师 Role: Melee Summoner

术士是憎恨之王扩展包中最具创新性的职业——他们并非传统意义上的远程法师，而是依靠恶魔随从进行战斗的近战召唤师。通过牺牲随从获得强大增益与爆发伤害，术士在当前版本中表现强势，是公认的S级职业。无论是单体Boss还是大规模AOE场景，献祭召唤流都能展现统治级的输出能力。 The Warlock is the most innovative class in Lord of Hatred — not a traditional ranged mage, but a melee summoner relying on demon minions. By sacrificing summons for powerful buffs and burst damage, Warlocks dominate the current meta as the undisputed S-tier class. Sacrifice Summoner build excels in both single-target Boss and large-scale AoE scenarios.

核心技能 Core Skills

完整技能树 Full Skill Tree

恶魔召唤 Demon Summon
Defense

召唤恶魔随从作战。可召唤地狱犬（冲锋）或痛苦魔（范围攻击）。随从继承部分角色属性。 Summon demon minions: Hell Hound (charge) or Torment Demon (AoE). Minions inherit partial stats.

召唤献祭 Summon Sacrifice
⚡ 核心机制

核心机制！牺牲所有随从获得30%增伤持续8秒，并立即对周围敌人造成基于随从最大生命的AOE伤害。献祭后刷新随从可再次触发。 Core mechanic! Sacrifice all minions for 30% damage boost for 8s and AoE damage based on minion max HP. Resummon to trigger again.

地狱烈焰 Hellfire Blast
Offense

主要AOE技能。释放地狱火对前方锥形区域造成持续伤害，命中被诅咒目标时额外触发一次爆炸。 Primary AoE skill. Release hellfire in a cone dealing continuous damage. Bonus explosion vs cursed targets.

诅咒印记 Curse Mark
Utility

标记目标使其受到伤害提高25%，持续10秒。被标记敌人死亡时诅咒会传播至周围敌人。 Mark target for 25% increased damage taken for 10s. Curse spreads to nearby enemies on kill.

暗蚀持续 Shadow Rot
Offense

DoT叠加技能。对目标施加暗蚀，每层造成持续伤害，最多叠加5层。层数越高伤害越高。 DoT stacking skill. Apply shadow rot layers, each dealing damage up to 5 stacks. Higher stacks = more damage.

灵魂虹吸 Soul Siphon
Defense

生命偷取技能。对目标造成伤害时恢复等量生命，击杀目标时额外获得一层暗蚀强化效果。 Life steal skill. Heal for damage dealt. Kill grants bonus shadow rot stack.

技能加点优先级 Skill Point Priority

  • 1 前期：恶魔召唤 + 召唤献祭（核心机制） Early: Demon Summon + Summon Sacrifice (core mechanic)
  • 2 中期：地狱烈焰 + 诅咒印记（AOE与增伤） Mid: Hellfire Blast + Curse Mark (AoE & damage boost)
  • 3 后期：暗蚀持续 + 灵魂虹吸（持续输出与续航） Late: Shadow Rot + Soul Siphon (sustained DPS & sustain)

属性优先级 Stat Priority

意志 Willpower → +随从增伤
力量 Strength → +基础伤害
活力 Vitality → +生存能力

推荐BD流派 Recommended Builds

🔥 献祭召唤流 ⭐ 推荐

术士最强流派！通过不断召唤-献祭循环实现高频爆发。献祭AOE伤害受益于随从最大生命，堆叠随从HP属性可大幅提升献祭伤害。大秘境刷图效率极高。 Warlock's strongest build! Continuous summon-sacrifice rotation for high-frequency burst. Sacrifice AoE scales with minion max HP. Excellent for Greater Rift farming.

版本之子Meta King S级 爆发AOEBurst AoE
💀 诅咒持续流

以诅咒印记和暗蚀为核心的DoT流派。诅咒传播机制实现自动AOE，叠加暗蚀层数造成持续高伤害。单体Boss战表现优异。 DoT-focused build with Curse Mark and Shadow Rot. Curse spread enables automatic AoE. Excellent single-target Boss performance.

持续伤害DoT Based A级 自动AOEAuto AoE
⚡ 地狱火爆发流

地狱烈焰为主输出的高爆发流派。配合献祭增伤Buff最大化地狱火伤害，适合快速击杀精英怪。操作相对简单，适合新手入门。 High-burst build centered on Hellfire Blast. Maximize damage with Sacrifice buff. Great for elite kills. Easy to play, good for beginners.
