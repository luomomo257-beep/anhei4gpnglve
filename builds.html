评级标准Rating Criteria

BD评级说明 Build Tier Rating System

以下评级基于综合考量：终局效率、生存能力、操作难度、通用性与版本环境适配度。 Ratings are based on: endgame efficiency, survivability, difficulty, versatility, and current patch meta.

S

版本顶级Meta-Defining

当前版本最强Build，效率远超其他选择。在所有终局场景中均有卓越表现，是追求极限玩家的首选。通常为版本之子，拥有碾压级的输出或辅助能力。 The absolute best builds in the current patch. Dominates all endgame content with overwhelming performance. These are the go-to picks for pushing limits — meta-defining power.

A

强力推荐Strong & Reliable

非常强力的Build，在对应场景中表现优异。可能需要一定装备投入，但成型后稳定高效，适合大多数玩家。在特定领域可能接近S级表现。 Very powerful builds that excel in their intended scenarios. May require some gear investment but perform consistently once built. Strong contenders in specific niches.

B

可用选择Viable Options

可以完成终局内容的Build，但效率不及S/A级。适合特定玩法偏好、新赛季起步或练号阶段。在版本平衡调整后可能晋升更高评级。 Builds that can clear endgame content but lack the efficiency of S/A tiers. Good for specific playstyles, fresh starts, or alt characters. May rise with future patches.

C

待加强Needs Buffs

当前版本表现偏弱的Build。在高层终局中存在明显短板，除非版本大幅调整或你对该流派有特殊偏好，否则不建议作为主力Build。 Underperforming builds in the current meta. Noticeable shortcomings in high-tier endgame. Avoid as main builds unless significantly buffed or you have a strong preference.

📋 当前版本BD环境 — 憎恨之王上线后 📋 Current Build Meta — After Lord of Hatred Launch

憎恨之王扩展包上线后，BD格局发生了重大变化。全新职业术士凭借召唤献祭体系一跃成为版本最强输出，圣骑士则以光环护卫体系成为团队副本不可或缺的核心。灵魂诞生者在终局清图效率上保持领先。传统老职业（野蛮人、死灵法师、法师、德鲁伊）在新职业的光芒下略显暗淡，但在特定场景中仍有一席之地。盗贼凭借暗影爆发流在高层苦痛中表现亮眼。当前版本整体倾向：术士 > 圣骑士 > 灵魂诞生者 > 盗贼 > 野蛮人 ≈ 死灵法师 > 法师 ≈ 德鲁伊。 The Lord of Hatred expansion dramatically reshaped the build meta. The new Warlock class leapt to the top with its Sacrifice Summon system, while Paladin's Aura Guardian became indispensable in group content. Spiritborn maintains its crown for speed-farming. Legacy classes (Barbarian, Necromancer, Sorcerer, Druid) took a back seat but still have niches. Rogue shines in high-tier Torment with Shadow burst builds. Overall tier: Warlock > Paladin > Spiritborn > Rogue > Barbarian ≈ Necromancer > Sorcerer ≈ Druid.

A级BD — 强力推荐 A-Tier Builds — Strong Picks

在特定场景中表现卓越，稳定高效的选择 Excellent in specific scenarios, reliable and efficient choices

#3 🔮
地狱火术士 Hellfire Warlock
🔮 Hellfire Warlock
术士 Warlock 持续输出 Sustained DPS 难度：中 Medium 战争计划 / 噩梦地下城 War Plans / Nightmare Dungeons

核心机制：地狱烈焰+诅咒DOT叠加。持续灼烧配合诅咒印记造成稳定的持续伤害，适合长线战斗。作为术士的第二主流派，操作比献祭流简单，但上限稍低。推荐程度仅次于献祭流，是术士玩家的备选方案。 Core Mechanic: Hellfire + Curse DoT stacking. Sustained burn damage combined with Curse Mark for stable DPS in extended fights. Simpler to play than the Sacrifice variant with a slightly lower ceiling. The second-best Warlock build — a solid alternative for Warlock mains.

#4 ⚔️
破盾圣骑 Shieldbreaker Paladin
⚔️ Shieldbreaker Paladin
圣骑士 Paladin 单体爆发 Single-Target Burst 难度：中 Medium 首领战 / 精英怪 Boss Fights / Elites

核心机制：神圣打击+盾牌格挡反击。专注于单体爆发输出，格挡成功后触发强力反击效果，对精英怪和BOSS造成毁灭性伤害。清图能力较弱，但在首领战场景中表现极为出色，是击杀BOSS的最佳Build之一。 Core Mechanic: Holy Strike + Shield Block Counterattack. Focused on single-target burst DPS — successful blocks trigger devastating counterattacks against elites and bosses. Weak at AoE clearing but excels in boss encounters, making it one of the best boss-killing builds available.

#5 🦅
猛禽流灵魂诞生者 Eagle Spiritborn
🦅 Eagle Spiritborn
灵魂诞生者 Spiritborn 高速清图 Speed Clear 难度：低 Easy 战争计划 / 地图探索 War Plans / Map Exploration

核心机制：鹰灵伙伴+高速位移。利用鹰灵伙伴的自动索敌能力和灵魂诞生者的高机动性实现极快清图。操作简单，容错率高。在憎恨之王新终局活动「战争计划」中效率极高，是刷战争计划的最适合BD，也是新手最易上手的高效Build。 Core Mechanic: Eagle spirit companion + high mobility. Leverages the Eagle's auto-targeting and Spiritborn's mobility for blazing-fast clear speeds. Easy to play with high fault tolerance. Extremely efficient in the new "War Plans" endgame activity — the best build for farming War Plans and the most beginner-friendly efficient build.

#6 🗡️
暗黑游侠 Shadow Rogue
🗡️ Shadow Rogue
盗贼 Rogue 爆发输出 Burst DPS 难度：高 Hard 高伤害要求场景 High DPS Check Scenarios

核心机制：暗影步+影袭爆发。利用潜行状态的高伤害倍率和影袭的瞬间爆发，在极短时间内倾泻巨量伤害。操作要求高，需要精准把握潜行时机和技能循环，但单体爆发伤害在所有Build中名列前茅。适合对高伤害输出有要求的所有场景。 Core Mechanic: Shadow Step + Shadow Strike burst. Leverages stealth damage multipliers and instant burst to dump massive damage in seconds. High skill cap requiring precise stealth timing and rotation management, but single-target burst damage ranks among the highest of all builds. Ideal for any content with tight DPS checks.

B

B级BD — 可用选择 B-Tier Builds — Viable Options

可以完成终局内容，但效率不及S/A级 Can clear endgame content, but less efficient than S/A tiers

#7 ⚒️
暴风野蛮人 Stormbane Barbarian
⚒️ Stormbane Barbarian
野蛮人 Barbarian 近战清怪 Melee Clear 难度：低 Easy 练号 / 新赛季起步 Leveling / Fresh Start

核心机制：旋风斩+暴风打击连携。经典近战清怪流派，操作简单粗暴。在新职业的光芒下略显暗淡，但练号和赛季初期的过渡阶段依然非常可靠。装备需求低，成型快，适合作为开荒Build使用。 Core Mechanic: Whirlwind + Storm Strike combo. A classic melee clearing build — simple and straightforward. Overshadowed by new classes but still very reliable for leveling and early-season transitions. Low gear requirements, fast to build, great as a starter build.

#8 💀
鲜血死灵 Blood Necromancer
💀 Blood Necromancer
死灵法师 Necromancer 生存输出 Survival DPS 难度：中 Medium

核心机制：鲜血虹吸+亡骸军团。通过吸血技能维持生存，亡骸军团提供稳定输出。在憎恨之王版本中受到一定削弱，输出效率不及新职业Build，但生存能力依然出色。适合偏好稳健打法的玩家，在高层苦痛中通过持久战消耗取胜。 Core Mechanic: Blood Siphon + Corpse Army. Sustain via lifesteal skills with consistent damage from undead minions. Slightly nerfed in Lord of Hatred, falling behind new class builds in raw output, but survivability remains excellent. Suited for steady playstyles — wins high Torment through attrition.

#9 ❄️
冰霜法师 Frost Sorcerer
❄️ Frost Sorcerer
法师 Sorcerer AOE控制 AOE Control 难度：中 Medium

核心机制：暴风雪+冰霜新星冻结控制。以冰系AOE和控制为核心，通过冻结敌人创造安全输出环境。输出能力在当前版本偏弱，但团队控制价值在组队副本中有一定发挥空间。适合喜欢控制型玩法的法师玩家。 Core Mechanic: Blizzard + Frost Nova freeze control. Built around ice AoE and crowd control, creating safe damage windows by freezing enemies. Raw output is below average in the current meta, but team control value has some use in group content. Suited for Sorcerer players who enjoy control-oriented playstyles.

#10 🌲
自然德鲁伊 Nature Druid
🌲 Nature Druid
德鲁伊 Druid 变形战 Shapeshift Combat 难度：高 Hard

核心机制：人熊形态切换+自然之力。通过灵活切换人形和熊形态，配合自然之力技能在两种形态间切换输出模式。操作难度最高，但收益不成正比。在憎恨之王版本中表现低迷，需要等待版本平衡调整。仅推荐给德鲁伊忠实玩家。 Core Mechanic: Human/Bear shapeshift + Nature's Force. Alternates between humanoid and bear forms with Nature's Force toggling damage modes. Highest difficulty but disproportionate rewards. Underperforming in the Lord of Hatred patch — likely needs balance adjustments. Only recommended for dedicated Druid mains.

核心词缀Core Affixes 主要输出词缀Primary DPS Affixes 根据BD类型选择对应的输出加成词缀（如献祭伤害、暗影伤害、物理伤害等）。这些词缀直接决定Build的输出上限，优先级最高。每件装备至少保证1个核心词缀。 Select damage affixes matching your build type (e.g., Sacrifice Damage, Shadow Damage, Physical Damage, etc.). These directly determine your DPS ceiling — highest priority. Aim for at least 1 core affix per item.
重要词缀Important Affixes 生存/续航词缀Survival / Sustain Affixes 提升容错率的词缀，包括生命值上限、护甲、全元素抗性、格挡率、吸血等。在高难度场景中至关重要，建议在核心词缀满足后优先考虑。生存词缀不足会导致频繁死亡，严重影响效率。 Affixes that improve error tolerance: Max Life, Armor, All Res, Block Rate, Lifesteal, etc. Crucial in high-difficulty content — prioritize after core DPS affixes. Insufficient survivability leads to frequent deaths, severely impacting efficiency.
