S13 清算赛季S13 Season of Reckoning

憎恨纪元的最终清算时刻。斯科沃斯大陆被憎恨之力侵扰，英雄们必须在这片土地上证明自己的价值，击败彼列，阻止墨菲斯托的复活。 The final reckoning of the Age of Hatred. Sanctuary's heroes must prove their worth on Skovos, defeat Belial, and prevent Mephisto's resurrection.

🏆 S13赛季重大变化 Major S13 Changes

⬆️

等级上限提升Level Cap Raised

等级上限从50级提升至70级，给予玩家更长的成长曲线。前中期内容更丰富，新手有更舒适的体验窗口。 Level cap raised from 50 to 70, creating a longer progression curve. More mid-game content, better new player experience.

⚗️

余烬祝福系统Ember Blessing System

新系统！通过每日/每周活动获取余烬材料，激活5种不同祝福，最多叠加至5级，大幅提升资源获取效率。 New system! Earn Ember materials from daily/weekly activities to activate 5 different blessings, stackable to level 5, boosting resource efficiency.

🗺️

斯科沃斯专属内容Skovos-Exclusive Content

清算试炼、憎恨渗透事件、斯科沃斯探索赛等独有活动，完成可获得专属奖励与余烬材料。 Exclusive activities like Reckoning Trials, Hatred Infiltration Events, and Skovos Exploration Races with unique rewards and Ember materials.

🎫

战网通行证优化Battle Pass Improvements

S13通行证增加了更多外观奖励，包括斯科沃斯主题坐骑、护甲套装、传送门等。免费版也提供基础奖励。 S13 Battle Pass adds more cosmetic rewards including Skovos-themed mounts, armor sets, portals. Even the free track has solid rewards.

📈 等级上限提升至 70 级 Level Cap Raised to Level 70

70

前所未有的成长空间Unprecedented Growth Space

S13将最高等级由50提升至70，给予玩家更长的成长曲线。这意味着前中期内容更为丰富，不会像往届赛季那样一冲就直接进终局。新手玩家这次有更舒适的体验窗口。 S13 raises the max level from 50 to 70, creating a longer progression curve. Players won't rocket straight into endgame — giving new players a far more comfortable experience window. More skill points to experiment with builds.

等级分布与技能点分配建议 Level Distribution & Skill Point Allocation

🌱

1-40级：开荒期Lv 1-40: Early Game

跟随主线剧情推进斯科沃斯地图。建议优先点满1-2个主要输出技能，其余随意。装备更换频繁，不要过度投资。 Follow the main story through Skovos. Prioritize maxing 1-2 main damage skills. Gear changes frequently — don't over-invest early.

⚔️

40-60级：中期成型Lv 40-60: Mid Game

开始锁定BD方向，系统性投入技能点。激活赫拉迪姆方块，开始尝试制作系统。战利品过滤调整为稀有以上。 Lock in your build direction. Systematically invest skill points. Activate Horadric Cube, experiment with crafting. Set loot filter to Rare+.

🏆

60-70级：终局冲刺Lv 60-70: Endgame Push

完成所有技能点投入。开始刷祖先级装备。激活余烬祝福系统。解锁战争计划开始定制化终局玩法。 Complete all skill point investments. Start farming Ancestral gear. Activate Ember Blessing system. Unlock War Plans for customized endgame.

💡 购买建议Purchase Recommendations

如果你是赛季玩家并希望收集外观，建议直接购买豪华版（包含高级+额外内容）。高级版性价比最高，适合大多数玩家。免费版内容虽然不多，但200白金币是实打实的资源，不购买通行证也能获得。 If you're a seasonal player who collects cosmetics, go Deluxe (includes Premium + extra). Premium offers the best value for most players. Free track content is limited but the 200 Platinum is solid currency even without purchase.

⚗️ 余烬祝福系统 Ember Blessings Ember Blessings

消耗每日/每周活动获得的余烬材料激活祝福，最高可叠加至5级。建议优先激活推荐等级满的祝福。 Spend Ember materials earned from daily/weekly activities to activate blessings, stackable up to level 5. Prioritize the recommended blessings first.

🏺 回收之瓮Salvage Urn ⭐推荐Priority

提高稀有材料拆解出率约30%/级。终局玩家核心祝福，大幅提升材料获取效率。 Increases rare material salvage rate by ~30% per level. Core blessing for endgame players — massive material efficiency boost.

1 2 3 4 5
⚒️ 大师之瓮Artisan's Urn ⭐推荐Priority

提高黑曜石掉落率约25%/级。黑曜石是制作传说装备的核心材料，终局必备。 Increases Obducite drop rate by ~25% per level. Obducite is essential for crafting legendary gear — essential for endgame.

1 2 3 4 5
❓ 好奇之瓮Curiosity's Urn

提高从杂货商处获得双件物品的概率约20%/级。适合经常在商人处刷新购买装备的玩家。 Increases dual-item chances from merchants by ~20% per level. Good for players who frequently buy gear from merchants.

1 2 3 4 5
🔮 雕文之瓮Glyph Urn

有概率（15%/级）获得额外雕文强化效果。适合已完成大部分技能点投入、开始关注巅峰雕文的玩家。 15% chance per level to gain bonus Glyph enhancements. Ideal for players who have most skill points invested and focus on Paragon Glyphs.

1 2 3 4 5
👻 祖先低语之瓮Ancestral Urn

提高获得祖先低语缓存概率约20%/级。对快速获取祖先级装备有帮助，适合刷装阶段。 Increases Ancestral Whisper Cache drop rate by ~20% per level. Helps with fast Ancestral gear acquisition — great during gear-farming phase.

推荐激活顺序 Recommended Activation Order

1️⃣

第一优先First Priority

回收之瓮 5级 + 大师之瓮 5级。这两个祝福直接提升制作和材料获取效率，是终局最核心的祝福组合。 Salvage Urn Lv5 + Artisan's Urn Lv5. These two blessings directly boost crafting and material efficiency — the core endgame combo.

2️⃣

第二优先Second Priority

祖先低语之瓮 3级。帮助快速获取祖先级装备，为后续制作打下基础。 Ancestral Urn Lv3. Helps quickly acquire Ancestral gear for crafting foundations.

3️⃣

第三优先Third Priority

好奇之瓮 3级。如果经常从商人购买装备，这个祝福效果明显。 Curiosity Urn Lv3. If you frequently buy gear from merchants, this blessing has noticeable impact.

⚔️ S13赛季特有活动 S13 Exclusive Seasonal Activities

⚔️

清算试炼 Daily Reckoning TrialDaily Reckoning Trial

每日挑战，完成可获得3-5个回响痕迹和余烬材料。是回荡之憎入场券的主要来源，建议每天都完成。 Daily challenge. Completing grants 3-5 Echoing Traces and Ember materials. Main source of Echoing Hatred tickets — complete daily.

🌪️

憎恨渗透事件 Hatred InfiltrationHatred Infiltration Event

限时高难度事件，在斯科沃斯随机地点触发。击败渗透的憎恨首领可获得大量余烬材料和斯科沃斯特有奖励。 Timed high-difficulty event, triggered at random Skovos locations. Defeating the infiltration Hatred boss grants large amounts of Ember materials and Skovos-exclusive rewards.

🏃

斯科沃斯探索赛 Skovos RaceSkovos Exploration Race

计时挑战，在限定时间内探索斯科沃斯指定区域并完成目标。排行榜竞争激烈，完成可获得赛季旅程终极奖励。 Timed challenge to explore Skovos regions and complete objectives within a time limit. Competitive leaderboards. Completion grants Season Journey ultimate rewards.

👑

每周首领挑战 Weekly Boss ChallengeWeekly Boss Challenge

每周重置，击败指定BOSS获得15-20个回响痕迹和大量余烬材料。是余烬祝福升级的主要途径。 Resets weekly. Defeating the designated boss grants 15-20 Echoing Traces and large amounts of Ember materials. Primary path for Ember Blessing upgrades.
