💀 回荡之憎 Echoing Hatred Echoing Hatred

高风险终局试炼。需从日常活动积攒稀有资源"回响痕迹"解锁入场，每次挑战随机敌人与遭遇设定，专门考验极限BD。是获取祖先级装备和神话装备概率最高的活动之一。 High-stakes endgame trial. Requires rare "Echoing Traces" earned from daily activities. Each run features randomized enemies and encounters designed to stress-test optimized builds.

🔑

入场条件与准备Entry Requirements & Preparation

获取回响痕迹 · 泰米斯城活动中心Earn Echoing Traces · Temis Activity Hub

如何获取回响痕迹How to Earn Echoing Traces

  • 📅 每日清算试炼Daily Reckoning Trial — 完成可获得3-5个回响痕迹 Complete for 3–5 Echoing Traces
  • ⚔️ 每周首领挑战Weekly Boss Challenge — 完成可获得15-20个回响痕迹 Complete for 15–20 Echoing Traces
  • 🌟 斯科沃斯特有活动Skovos-Specific Events — 随机事件额外奖励 Random event bonus rewards
  • 🎫 赛季祝福加成Season Blessing Bonus — 激活"回收之瓮"可增加获取量 Activate Salvage Urn to boost gains

随机事件机制（每次进入随机生成） Random Event Mechanics (Generated Per Run)

1
随机敌人组合Random Enemy Composition
系统随机从斯科沃斯的敌人池中选取组合。可能全近战、高护甲精英群、或远程法爷队。挑战前观察入口处提示。 System randomly selects from Skovos enemy pool. Could be all-melee, armored elites, or ranged caster squads. Check entrance hints before entering.
2
随机环境效果Random Environmental Effects
中毒地面、冰冻区域、火焰池、强风推人等随机环境惩罚。要求BD有应对手段或灵活走位。 Poison ground, frozen zones, flame puddles, wind push — requires builds with countermeasures or flexible kiting.
3
随机BOSS介入Random Boss Interruption
深层（30层以上）会随机有BOSS介入战斗。都瑞尔、憎恨先驱、安达利尔回响都可能在关键时刻出现。 At deep levels (30+), bosses can interrupt at any time. Duriel, Hatred Vanguard, or Andariel Echo may appear at critical moments.

♻️ 资源回收Resource Salvage

分解多余材料获取有用材料 Break down excess materials for useful ones

可将多余套装材料、远古材料等转换为精华或其他制作材料。激活"回收之瓮"祝福可提升产出量约30%。 Convert excess set mats, ancient materials into essence or crafting mats. Activating Salvage Urn blessing boosts yield by ~30%.

📿 符咒提取Rune Extraction

从装备中提取符咒 Extract Runes from socketed gear

将已镶嵌符咒的装备放入方块，可提取符咒并保留装备。提取成功率100%，无材料消耗。 Place rune-socketed gear in the cube to extract runes while keeping the item. 100% success rate, no material cost.

📿 护身符 & 符咒系统 Amulets & Runes Amulets & Runes

符咒作为战利品掉落并镶嵌于护身符，根据组合激活套装效果。稀有套装符咒提供极强加成，是后期BD优化的核心系统。 Runes drop as loot and socket into Amulets, activating set bonuses based on combinations. Rare Set Runes provide powerful bonuses critical to late-game build optimization.

🔍 战利品过滤器 Loot Filter Loot Filter

千呼万唤始出来！玩家社区期盼多年的战利品过滤器终于实装。自定义地面物品显示方式，大幅提升刷宝效率，减少视觉杂乱。 After years of community requests, the Loot Filter is finally here! Customize ground item display to massively improve farming efficiency and reduce visual clutter.

⚔️ 其他终局活动简述Other Endgame Activities

🏚️
噩梦地下城 Nightmare DungeonsNightmare Dungeons
通过完成普通地下城解锁。难度由地下城等级决定，词缀系统增加随机挑战。憎恨之王新增了斯科沃斯特有词缀。 Unlocked by completing normal dungeons. Difficulty scales with dungeon level. New Skovos-specific affixes added in Lord of Hatred.
🌳
低语之树 Tree of WhispersTree of Whispers
40秒循环奖励活动。完成各种目标获取低语币，可兑换奖励袋。憎恨之王版本提升了奖励池并增加了斯科沃斯专属奖励。 40-second loop activity. Complete objectives for Whisper coins to redeem reward bags. Lord of Hatred improved the reward pool with Skovos-exclusive rewards.
🌪️
地狱大军 HelltideHelltide
斯科沃斯限时事件，需收集腐蚀碎片开启箱子。憎恨之王增加了更高难度的地狱狂潮版本，并提升了神话装备在箱子中的掉率。 Timed event in Skovos, requires Corrupted Fragments to open chests. Lord of Hatred added higher-difficulty Helltide variants and improved Mythic chest rates.
👻
祖先召唤 Ancestral SummonAncestral Summon
召唤池挑战，随机出现强力祖先敌人。击败后获得祖先精华和稀有材料。是刷取神话碎片的重要途径。 Summoning pool challenge with random powerful Ancestral enemies. Defeating them grants Ancestral Essence and rare materials — key for Mythic Fragment farming.
