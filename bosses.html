系统概述System Overview

憎恨之王BOSS系统 Lord of Hatred Boss System

憎恨之王扩展包对BOSS系统进行了全面革新，引入阶梯式挑战结构与简化后的钥匙系统 Lord of Hatred overhauled the boss system with tiered challenges and simplified key mechanics

📊

阶梯式挑战结构Tiered Challenge Structure

初级巢穴（Tier 1）→ 高级巢穴（Tier 2）→ 彼列巅峰挑战。三级难度递进，掉落品质逐级提升。 Tier 1 → Tier 2 → Belial Apex. Three difficulty tiers with increasing loot quality.

🔑

钥匙系统简化Simplified Key System

击败BOSS直接掉落钥匙，无需囤积材料。挑战更流畅，farm更高效。 Defeating bosses drops keys directly. No material hoarding required. Smoother gameplay.

⚔️

通用战斗准备Combat Preparation

高生命值上限、解控技能、元素抗性药水、足够输出。熟悉BOSS机制是通关关键。 High HP pool, CC break, elemental resist potions, sufficient damage. Knowing boss mechanics is key.

👥

组队加成机制Co-op Bonus System

每增加一名队友，暗金掉落概率+33%。4人组队效率最高，尤其适合彼列巅峰挑战。 Each additional party member adds +33% Mythic drop rate. 4-player teams are most efficient.
