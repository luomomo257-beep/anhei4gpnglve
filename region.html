斯科沃斯群岛 Skovos Isles

位于庇护之地东南方向，由多座群岛组成的神秘大陆。这里常年被风暴笼罩，地狱腐化正在蔓延。 A mysterious continent of islands southeast of Sanctuary, constantly shrouded in storms with demonic corruption spreading.

🏰

主城泰米斯 Temis City

斯科沃斯的中央枢纽城市，所有终局活动的出发点。城内设有铁匠、商贩、任务发布者等关键NPC。 The central hub of Skovos, serving as the starting point for all endgame activities. Key NPCs include Blacksmith, Merchants, and Quest Givers.

气候环境 Climate & Environment

风暴海岸 Stormbound Coast

Stormbound Coast · 常年风暴侵袭的群岛海岸

风暴海岸是斯科沃斯群岛的门户区域，广阔的沿海地带被永恒的风暴不断侵蚀。海浪日夜拍打着嶙峋的悬崖峭壁，形成了独特的地形景观。这片区域是大多数玩家首次探索斯科沃斯的起点，相对温和的气候和开阔的地形使其成为理想的练级区域。 The Stormbound Coast serves as the gateway to Skovos Isles. This vast coastal area is constantly eroded by eternal storms. Waves crash against rugged cliffs day and night, creating unique terrain. Most players begin their Skovos exploration here, where the relatively milder climate and open terrain make it ideal for leveling.

迷雾密林 Shrouded Forest

Shrouded Forest · 浓雾笼罩的神秘林地

迷雾密林是斯科沃斯最神秘的区域之一，终年被浓稠的迷雾所笼罩，能见度极低。这片古老的森林充满了腐化的树精、暗影狼群和游荡的迷雾幽魂。玩家必须时刻保持警觉，因为敌人经常从雾中突然发起伏击。森林深处隐藏着珍贵的宝箱和神秘的秘密BOSS。 The Shrouded Forest is one of Skovos' most mysterious zones, permanently covered in thick fog with extremely low visibility. This ancient forest is filled with corrupted treants, shadow wolf packs, and wandering mist wraiths. Players must stay alert as enemies frequently ambush from within the fog. Hidden chests and mysterious secret bosses await in the forest depths.

古代废墟 Ancient Ruins

Ancient Ruins · 远古文明的石质遗迹

古代废墟是斯科沃斯最具探索价值的区域，这里保存着失落远古文明的遗迹。巨大的石质建筑、精密的机关系统、以及被遗忘的宝藏吸引着无数冒险者。石像鬼守卫守护着这些遗迹，诅咒亡魂和远古祭司在废墟中游荡。地下城密集分布，是获取传说装备的最佳地点。 The Ancient Ruins are the most explorable area in Skovos, preserving remnants of a lost ancient civilization. Massive stone structures, intricate trap systems, and forgotten treasures attract countless adventurers. Gargoyle guardians protect these ruins while cursed spirits and ancient priests wander the grounds. Dense dungeon distribution makes this the best location for legendary gear.

熔火高地 Volcanic Highlands

Volcanic Highlands · 地狱腐化最严重的区域

熔火高地是斯科沃斯最危险的区域，火山活动频繁，熔岩河流纵横交错。这是地狱腐化蔓延最严重的地区，熔岩巨人、火焰恶魔和憎恨领主在此盘踞。高风险带来高收益，这里精英怪密集，是追求顶级装备玩家的必刷之地。火山核心隐藏着斯科沃斯最强大的秘密。 The Volcanic Highlands are Skovos' most dangerous zone with frequent volcanic activity and crisscrossing lava rivers. This is the most severely hell-corrupted area, where Lava Giants, Fire Demons, and Hatred Lords lurk. High risk brings high reward with dense elite enemies—mandatory farming ground for players seeking top-tier gear. The Volcanic Core hides Skovos' most powerful secrets.
